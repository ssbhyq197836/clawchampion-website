FAQ

Quick answers for new players.

1) How do I log in?
You can log in via Guest Login, Gmail email registration, or social account login. We recommend using a frequently used email for announcements and events.
2) How do I play the coin pusher?
Insert coins to push coins forward. Dropped coins are added to your GP. You may also win JP Rewards or Full-board Rewards.
3) How do I play arcade games?
Enter an arcade room and follow the in-room instructions. Each game has different rules (e.g. pinball, fighting). Check the instructions before starting.

If you encounter black screen or severe stutter, try restarting the room or app. If needed, contact support with screenshots/video at customerservice@clawchampion.com.
7) How do I get GP and what is GP used for?
New users receive GP on first login; you can also get GP via invitations, recharge, and occasional events. GP is used for coin pusher, arcade games, and in-game rewards.

Please send your payment screenshot and game ID to customerservice@clawchampion.com. We will verify and compensate if needed.
