FAQ

Quick answers

How do I log in to ClawChampion?
You can log in via Guest Login, Gmail Email Registration, or Social Account Login.
How do I play the coin pusher?
Insert coins to push forward. Dropped coins are added to your GP, and you may trigger JP or Full-board rewards.
What should I do if the game has operation issues?
Tap the in-room “Fault” button. Our ops team is online 24/7. If you lost GP due to faults, provide screenshots/videos for verification.
Recharge not credited — what should I do?
Email customerservice@clawchampion.com with your payment screenshot and game ID for verification.
Important: ClawChampion is a pure entertainment game. No physical prizes, shipping or logistics services are provided.
